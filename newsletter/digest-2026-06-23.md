### Shielded Labs, ZODL, Zcash Foundation Updates
- **ZODL**, **Shielded Labs**, **Zcash Foundation**, Tachyon Group and Valar Group continue work on the **Ironwood** upgrade. This new shielded pool with turnstile accounting will allow anyone to independently verify the ZEC supply. Target activation: late July 2026.  
  [Ironwood: A New Shielded Pool for Zcash](https://zodl.com/ironwood-a-new-shielded-pool-for-zcash/)

- The emergency network upgrade (NU6.2) successfully fixed the critical soundness bug in the Orchard pool. User funds and privacy were protected.  
  [Zcash Foundation – Zebra 4.5.3 and 5.0.0 Emergency Soft Fork](https://zfnd.org/zebra-4-5-3-and-5-0-0-emergency-soft-fork-and-nu6-2-activation/)

- **ZODL** released updates for the **Zodl** mobile wallet (formerly Zashi), including multi-currency fiat display, improved server selection, and better performance.
