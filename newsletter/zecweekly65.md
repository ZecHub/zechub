#### Welcome to ZecWeekly

Hello Zcash world! Welcome to the weekly update on the latest within the Zcash Community and the broader crypto landscape.

This week covers the successful ZF Minor grants projects, updates from ECC and Bitcoin core developer steping back from Lightning Network, and finally we bring zcash closer for your every online purchase.

Stay informed!


#### Education Piece of the week 

This week ZecHub released a new tutorial demonstrating how an online VPN subscription package can be purchased using Zcash. The demo features TorGuard VPN and Zingo Desktop wallet. It is absolutely possible to store your wealth privately in Zcash and purchase everyday items!
