# Zcash Ecosystem Digest | June 27th

Ironwood consensus rules finalized for late July. Anthropic Mythos found no new critical bugs in Zcash. Keystone confirms day-one Ironwood support. ZCG election voting open: 14 candidates for 2 seats. Zallet Alpha 4 gets closer. New grant proposals for ZPAD-20 and zebra-chain formal verification.

Curated by sable apathy

## ZODL, Zcash Foundation & Shielded Labs 🛠️, and Zcash Updates 🛠️

[Ironwood: A New Shielded Pool for Zcash - @zodl_app](https://zodl.com/ironwood-a-new-shielded-pool-for-zcash/) — ZODL, Tachyon, Valar Group, the Zcash Foundation, and Shielded Labs proposed Ironwood together. It's a new shielded pool that uses the corrected Orchard circuit and adds turnstile supply verification. Target is late July 2026, right after zcashd hits end-of-support at block 3,417,100.

[Ironwood: Auditing the Orchard Pool's Supply - @project_tachyon](https://tachyon.z.cash/blog/auditing-orchard-supply/) — Tachyon breaks down how the turnstile works. Every Orchard fund passes through a verifiable checkpoint before it enters Ironwood. Users can verify the circulating supply themselves. No third party needed.

[Zcash Ecosystem Approves Ironwood Upgrade Consensus Rules - @crowdfundinsider](https://www.crowdfundinsider.com/2026/06/284478-zcash-zec-ecosystem-approves-ironwood-upgrade-consensus-rules-aims-for-late-july-activation/) — The final consensus rule changes for Ironwood are settled. Work now shifts to implementation, wallet integration, and outreach to exchanges and infrastructure providers.

[Keystone Confirms Day-One Ironwood Support - @KeystoneWallet](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/keystone-ironwood-support/56170) — Keystone's next firmware supports Ironwood from day one, no later than July 3rd. ValarDragon confirmed the migration flow already works on testnet with VizorWallet and Keystone. TEX and T3 address support is coming in the Nexus app too.

[Irrational Thinking. ZODL Update - @joshs](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/irrational-thinking-zodl-update/56211) — Zodl 3.5.2 and 3.5.3 are out: coinholder polling UI cleanup, iOS Swap and Pay error fixed, old Stardust servers removed. Zodl 3.6 brings auto and manual server selection plus multi-currency conversion. Orchard-to-Ironwood migration work has started. PGPZ (Pretty Good Policy for Zcash) is live with its site and community.

[Subduing the World. ZODL Update - @joshs](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/subduing-the-world-update-from-zodl/56072) — Josh Swihart walked through the 50-hour emergency response to the Orchard bug. The soft fork hit block 3,363,426, then NU6.2 hard-forked at 3,364,600. Coinholder Polling 3.5.0 landed on Android, finishing the first cross-platform in-wallet shielded voting launch.

[ZF Engineering Update June 1-14 - @ZcashFoundation](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/zf-engineering-update-june-1-14-2026/56249) — A busy two weeks. ZF fixed a sync stall in initial block download and shipped Zebra v5.0.0 through v5.1.1 with fresh checkpoints. The minimum peer protocol got bumped to NU6.2 too.

[ZF Engineering Update May 18-31 - @ZcashFoundation](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/zf-engineering-update-may-18-31-2026/55965) — Zebra 4.5.0 and 4.5.1 landed with 13 security fixes. One was a consensus-critical P2SH sigop issue. ZIP-213 (Shielded Coinbase) shipped — miners can now send rewards straight to a shielded address.

[Anthropic Mythos AI Audits Entire Zcash Protocol - @zooko](https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/t/zcash-protocol-reviewed-with-mythos/56183) — Shielded Labs asked Anthropic to run Mythos against the full Zcash protocol. No additional bugs turned up beyond the already-patched Orchard issue. The audit finished just before US export controls blocked access to Mythos and Fable 5.

[Anthropic's Secret AI Model Mythos Audits Entire Zcash Protocol - @BTCServices](https://news.bitcoin.com/anthropics-secret-ai-model-mythos-audits-entire-zcash-protocol-finds-no-new-bugs-37856/) — A closer look at the Mythos findings. Zooko thanked Anthropic publicly and said Shielded Labs and others are pressing on with security work ahead of Ironwood.

[Arborist Call #126 - Zcash Protocol Updates - @ZecHub](https://zechub.substack.com/p/arborist-call-zcash-protocol-updates-51f) — The June 11 call covered Zallet Alpha 4 (sync engine refactor, full block scanning, coinbase RPC), Ironwood development, formal verification, and Zebra security releases. Late July is still the target for zcashd deprecation.
