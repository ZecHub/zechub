# Creators & Tipping

Zcash provides tools for creators to receive tips and payments from their audience.

## TipZ

**Website:** [tipz.io](https://tipz.io)

A tipping platform that allows creators to receive Zcash tips from their supporters.

- **Type:** Tipping Platform
- **Features:** 
  - Social Tipping
  - Creator Profiles
  - Anonymous Tips
  - Instant Transfers
  - Zcash Support

---

## Zcash.me

**Website:** [zcash.me](https://zcash.me)

A simple payment link generator that allows anyone to request Zcash payments.

- **Type:** Payment Link Generator
- **Features:**
  - Tip Pages
  - Payment Requests
  - QR Codes
  - Custom Amounts
  - Note/Memo Support
