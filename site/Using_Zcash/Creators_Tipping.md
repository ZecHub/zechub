# Zcash Tools for Creators: Tipping & Profile Platforms

Zcash provides privacy-preserving tools for creators to receive tips and payments from their audience. Two key platforms — **Zcash.me** and **TipZ** — make it simple for content creators, cypherpunks, and the privacy-conscious community to accept support without exposing financial details.

---

## Zcash.me

**Website:** [zcash.me](https://zcash.me/)
**Twitter:** [@zcashme](https://x.com/zcashme)
**Profile Format:** `zcash.me/[username]`

### Overview

Zcash.me is a profile directory and discovery platform — often described as the "Linktree for Zcash." It gives every Zcash user a single shareable link where supporters can find their shielded address, social profiles, and bio. With over 1,600 registered users, it serves as a hub connecting infrastructure operators, developers, content creators, and community builders.

### Key Features

- **Custom Profile Pages** — Create a personalized page with your username, bio, avatar, and location. Share it with `zcash.me/[username]`.
- **Shielded Address Display** — Show your Zcash unified address with a one-click copy button so supporters can send ZEC directly.
- **Social Link Integration** — Connect and display links to X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Telegram, GitHub, and custom websites.
- **Verification System** — Earn a verified badge on your profile to build trust within the community.
- **Referral Leaderboard** — Track top profiles by referral activity and identify community leaders.
- **Activity Status** — Profiles show when a user was last active, helping supporters find engaged creators.

### Upcoming Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Location Map | Find Zcash users in your region for local community building |
| Platform Statistics | View platform-wide metrics and growth data |
| Community Polls | Vote on Zcash ecosystem matters |
| Forum | Discuss ideas with the community |
| Mobile App | Full-featured mobile experience |

### Getting Started with Zcash.me

1. Visit [zcash.me](https://zcash.me/) and click "Join"
2. Choose a unique username and add your bio and profile picture
3. Paste your Zcash unified address (starting with `u1...`) from your wallet
4. Connect your social accounts (X/Twitter, LinkedIn, Telegram, etc.)
5. Share `zcash.me/[yourusername]` on your social media, websites, and content

## TipZ

**Website:** [tipz.cash](https://tipz.cash/)
**Twitter:** [@tipz_cash](https://x.com/tipz_cash)
**Profile Format:** `tipz.cash/@[username]`

### Overview

TipZ is a non-custodial, private tipping protocol purpose-built for creators and cypherpunks. Fans can tip creators in any major cryptocurrency — ETH, USDC, USDT, SOL, or ZEC — and the payment is automatically converted and delivered as **shielded ZEC**. This ensures complete financial privacy for both sender and receiver. Creators keep 100% of every tip with zero platform fees.

### Key Features

#### Financial Freedom
- **0% Platform Fees** — Keep 100% of every tip received
- **Non-Custodial** — You control your funds at all times
- **Self-Custody** — Tips go directly to your wallet
- **No Middlemen** — Peer-to-peer payments only

#### Privacy-First Design
- **Shielded Transactions** — Every tip arrives as shielded ZEC, powered by Zcash zero-knowledge proof technology
- **Private Wallet Balance** — Supporters cannot see your total earnings or transaction history
- **Encrypted Messaging** — Send private messages alongside tips using shielded memos
- **No KYC Required** — Start receiving tips without identity verification

#### Cross-Chain Payments

TipZ accepts multiple cryptocurrencies that are automatically swapped to shielded ZEC:

| Currency | Network |
|----------|---------|
| ETH | Ethereum |
| USDC | Multiple chains |
| USDT | Multiple chains |
| SOL | Solana |
| ZEC | Direct Zcash |

#### Creator Protection
- **No Deplatforming Risk** — Decentralized protocol means no central authority can remove your account
- **Censorship Resistant** — Your ability to receive payments cannot be arbitrarily revoked

### How TipZ Works

1. **Creator registers** with their X handle at [tipz.cash](https://tipz.cash/)
2. **Creator receives** a unique payment link: `tipz.cash/@[handle]`
3. **Fan visits** the creator's tip page (no account required)
4. **Fan selects** a tip amount ($1, $5, $10, $25, or custom) and optional private message
5. **Fan pays** with ETH, USDC, USDT, SOL, or ZEC
6. **TipZ routes** the payment through cross-chain infrastructure and converts it to shielded ZEC
7. **Creator receives** shielded ZEC directly in their wallet (settles in approximately 5 minutes)

### Getting Started with TipZ

#### For Creators
1. Visit [tipz.cash](https://tipz.cash/) and sign in with your X/Twitter account
2. Add your Zcash shielded address from a wallet that supports unified addresses
3. Your unique link is ready: `tipz.cash/@[your-x-handle]`
4. Share your TipZ link in your X/Twitter bio, YouTube descriptions, newsletter footers, and website

#### For Supporters
1. Visit the creator's TipZ page (e.g., `tipz.cash/@creator`)
2. Select a tip amount
3. Choose your payment currency (ETH, USDC, USDT, SOL, or ZEC)
4. Add an optional encrypted message
5. Confirm the transaction — the creator receives shielded ZEC privately

## Why Privacy Matters for Creators

### The Problem with Transparent Crypto Tipping

When a fan sends a tip via Bitcoin or Ethereum, the transaction is fully visible on-chain:

| Issue | Impact |
|-------|--------|
| Transparent Balances | Anyone can see total earnings |
| Transaction History | All incoming/outgoing payments visible |
| Supporter Exposure | Fans' financial activity becomes linked to the creator |
| Targeted Attacks | High visible balances make creators a target |

### The Problem with Web2 Platforms

| Issue | Impact |
|-------|--------|
| High Fees | Patreon: 5-12%, Ko-fi: 0-5% + payment processing fees |
| Deplatforming | Accounts banned without warning |
| Data Harvesting | Supporter data sold or leaked |
| Geographic Restrictions | Services unavailable in many countries |

### The Zcash Solution

Zcash **shielded transactions** use zero-knowledge proofs to verify payments without revealing the sender address, receiver address, transaction amount, or wallet balance. This is battle-tested cryptography that has protected financial privacy since Zcash launched in 2016.

By combining Zcash.me for discovery and TipZ for private payments, creators gain a complete privacy-preserving support stack — no platform fees, no data harvesting, and no risk of deplatforming.
