# Mobile Top-up Using Zcash

In this guide, you'll learn how to buy airtime with ZEC.

## Follow these steps:

### 1. Create an account
Visit [Coinsbee.com](https://www.coinsbee.com/en/shop/mobile-top-ups/) and create an account.

### 2. Select your country and network
Scroll to the top left corner and select your country; then choose mobile phone credit and select the network that you'd like to top up (example: MTN).

![Step 2 - Selecting country and network](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/0*qvB7ZCWaVWa-C2d8)

### 3. Fill in details and purchase
Fill in the correct details and hit the "Buy Now" button. Open the cart to view your order, click continue, and confirm the purchase with $ZEC.

![Step 3 - Purchase screen](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/0*ikF-ooBc9W7olfNt)

### 4. Payment details
The equivalent amount in $ZEC will be provided, along with a transparent address where the funds should be sent.

![Step 4 - ZEC payment details](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/0*6_YhAhQnsLbrMvHN)

### 5. Send the payment
Copy and paste the amount and address carefully. In the memo field, add your YWallet address in case there will be a refund.

![Step 5 - Sending ZEC](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/0*DNnpyemsKcW-k-u-)

### 6. Processing
Once the funds have been sent, your request will be processed within a few minutes.

![Step 6 - Processing screen](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/0*LgXuUVLVecqrUW5G)

### 7. Confirmation
Hey… My line has been credited! You could also confirm your email during the payment process so you'll get notified via email once the transaction is completed.

![Step 7 - Successful top-up](https://cdn-images-1.medium.com/max/1200/0*TJKIcqznQ4Y8_jgo)
