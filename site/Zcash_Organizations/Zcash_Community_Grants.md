## Mission Statement

The Zcash Community Grants (ZCG) program funds independent teams entering the Zcash ecosystem to perform major ongoing development and other work that serves the public good of the Zcash ecosystem.

## People and Teams

Zcash Community Grants is managed by a committee currently consisting of five members. This committee reviews and approves grant applications from third-party teams building on the Zcash protocol.  
Learn more about the committee on the [Team page](https://zcashcommunitygrants.org/committee/).

Recent projects approved by ZCG include:

- **Zcash Shielded Assets (ZSA)** led by the QEDIT team, enabling DeFi functionality on Zcash through a new payments protocol with additional features for the Zcash mainnet.
- **Zcash Media Short Documentary**, featuring notable Zcash contributors such as Edward Snowden, Zooko, and Deirdre Connolly, created to deliver relevant, educational, and shareable content.
- **Ledger hardware wallet integration** by the Zondax team, enabling full support for Sapling shielded transactions for secure and private Zcash storage.
- **ZGo Point-of-Sale SDK**, a one-click shielded payment and point-of-sale system designed for brick-and-mortar merchants.
- **The Global Ambassador Program**, which expands Zcashâ€™s international representation and supports in-person community events worldwide.

Several additional projects have been reviewed by the Zcash Community Grants Committee. View the [full list of submitted projects](https://zcashgrants.org/gallery/25215916-53ea-4041-a3b2-6d00c487917d).

## Organizational Structure

Zcash Community Grants operates as an independent organization, separate from both the Electric Coin Company and the Zcash Foundation.

## Funding

The Electric Coin Company receives 8% of the [Zcash Development Fund](https://zips.z.cash/zip-1014). These funds are used to support third-party development teams as well as internal operations.

## How to Apply for a Zcash Community Grant (ZCG)

Zcash Community Grants applications are submitted through GitHub and discussed on the Zcash Community Forum. Follow the steps below to apply.

### Step 1: Prepare Your Proposal

Ensure your proposal includes the following:

- Project overview
- Goals and deliverables
- Timeline and milestones
- Budget
- Team or contributor background

### Step 2: Submit on GitHub

1. Visit the Zcash Community Grants GitHub repository:  
   https://github.com/ZecHub/zechub/blob/main/site/Zcash_Organizations/Zcash_Community_Grants.md
2. Open the **Issues** tab and click **New Issue**.
3. Select the official ZCG proposal template.
4. Complete all required sections.
5. Submit the issue to publish your application.

### Step 3: Post on the Community Forum

1. Visit the Zcash Community Grants category on the forum:  
   https://forum.zcashcommunity.com/c/grants/33
2. Create a new topic.
3. Share a brief summary of your proposal and include a link to the GitHub issue.
4. Publish the post to invite community discussion.

### Step 4: Review Process

Applicants should actively monitor GitHub and the community forum for questions or feedback.

Final decisions and updates are communicated through these channels.
