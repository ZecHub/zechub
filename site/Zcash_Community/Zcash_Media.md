# Zcash Media

"Our role in this collaborative effort is to educate, inspire, and excite current and future Zcashers, building a base of supporters that rivals the largest projects and beyond."


## Background

Produced by [37 LAINES](https://www.37laines.com/) for the public good, Zcash Media's educational videos are all about Zcash and crypto. It is funded by the [Zcash Community Grants](https://x.com/ZcashCommGrants) program, and has become an important player in the Zcash ecosystem.
